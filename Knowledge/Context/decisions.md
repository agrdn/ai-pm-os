# Decision Log

All product decisions with context, reasoning, and key discussion points.

---

## How to Read This File

Each meeting entry includes:
- **Key Decisions** - What was decided and why
- **Discussion Points** - Important topics discussed (even if no decision yet)
- **Tensions & Tradeoffs** - Conflicts or competing priorities surfaced
- **Open Questions** - Things still unresolved
- **Context & Insights** - Background info and observations

---

## Meetings

*Newest first. Each meeting captures the full picture, not just decisions.*

*AI will populate this as you process meeting notes.*
